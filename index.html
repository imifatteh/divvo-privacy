Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Divvo app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by Imtiaz Fatteh (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information We Collect

To use the Application, you must create an account. During registration and use, the Application collects the following information:

  • Name – used to identify you to other users within shared bills
  • Email address – used for authentication and account management
  • Phone number – used to help other Divvo users find you by contact
  • User ID – a unique identifier tied to your account
  • Photos – receipt images you choose to upload are stored to enable bill scanning and sharing
  • User-generated content – bill data, items, and claims you create within the Application

The Application does not collect precise location information, advertising data, or usage analytics.

How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used solely to provide and improve the Application's functionality, including:

  • Authenticating your account
  • Displaying your name to other users in shared bills
  • Allowing other Divvo users to find you via your phone number
  • Storing and displaying receipt images and bill data

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or selling to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses Firebase (provided by Google) for authentication, data storage, and file storage. Your data is stored on Firebase's servers in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

The Application also enables optional payment links to third-party apps (Venmo, Cash App, PayPal, Zelle). We do not share your data with these services; linking to them simply opens their respective apps on your device.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You may delete your account at any time from within the Application's settings, which will permanently remove your personal information from our systems.

Children

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us at admin@divvoapp.com and we will delete it promptly.

Security

The Service Provider takes the security of your data seriously and relies on Firebase's industry-standard security infrastructure to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

This privacy policy is effective as of 2026-03-14.
